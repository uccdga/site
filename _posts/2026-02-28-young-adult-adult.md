**Sunday Morning Small Groups**  - Our **young adult/adult ministries** continue as scheduled. Our **Young Adults** **Group** continue through select passages in Romans, then onto Philippians. The **Bible Study Group** that meets in the Downstairs Dining Hall continues with their study on James. The new **Choir Group** meets on the second floor (Room 26), and is now following the sermon series in the Gospel of John. The **Parenting Group** continues to meet in the Fellowship Room, and are currently in a curriculum titled "The Art of Parenting".

**Mid-Week Connects**  - Our Ferris Hills group has been meeting monthly, which includes worship and a devotional. There are now conversations to move this to weekly. Quail Summit group continues, and will attempt to meet monthly through the end of the school year.

**Adult Ministry Bible Studies**  - As a reminder, we have a men's group that meets every Wednesday morning at 7am at the church for breakfast, fellowship, Bible study, and prayer. We also have a group of women that meet every Thursday at 1pm at the church - also a Bible study. The church calendar provides additional information for open events in the church.

**25-35-Somethings Small Group**  - We started this group in February - we meet weekly on Tuesdays, 4pm-5pm. This includes people who grew up in our church, and those who have not! We are using a curriculum called "Alpha" for a few weeks, and then move onto a study in Philippians.
