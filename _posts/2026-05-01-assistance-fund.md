On the first Sundays of May, loose offering will be designated for the Stewards Assistance Fund. This fund allows us to care for those in our church family in practical and meaningful ways during times of need. Your generosity helps us quietly extend compassion and support when it is needed most. If you would like to give directly to the Assistance Fund at any time, simply note "Assistance Fund" on your check or envelope.
