# **Care Team Ministries Volunteers: This One's for You**

If you serve on  **any Care Ministry team**, this invitation is for you!

You are warmly invited to a  **Care Team Ministries Potluck**  on  **Sunday, March 15, from 12-2 p.m.**  in the  **United Church Dining Hall**.

This gathering is for anyone who serves on a Care Ministry team, including:  **Stewards, Care Ministers, Transportation, Meal Train, Visitation, Hospitality, Card Ministry, Support Team, and Prayer Shawl**.

Come enjoy good food, meaningful conversation, and a short, creative time of interaction centered on caring for others -- all woven together with fellowship, laughter, and connection.

**_This gathering is our way of saying thank you for the many ways you faithfully care for others within our church family._**
