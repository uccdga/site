**Sunday School** - Our **Middle School and High School groups** continue. MS/HS activity time at the beginning of Sunday School also continues (it has been received well). Both groups continue to use the LIVE curriculum, with Middle School going through "Bible 101" and “Growing Closer to Each Other”; and High School going through "God wants to Restore You" and "The Ordinary Becomes the Extraordinary".

**High School Youth Group (The Cause)**  - We have moved onto Philippians and then onto Revelation in our studies. We are currently using a small group format, with large group sharing and teaching at the end. Our youth band continues to practice and grow, with plans for Sunday morning worship!

**Youth Club Middle School Group Bible Study**  - This class is following the same studies as the high school group. We have recently changed how we conduct our Bible study; now, we move the students upstairs early for announcements, and then into a longer Bible study, splitting the group into 4 small groups, before ending with large group sharing and teaching (like the HS'ers). We are thankful for our facilitators (Ed Blance, Jen Callard, and Lauren Slomski).

**School District Connects**  - Our mid-week connects continue, with Victor, Marcus Whitman, and Canandaigua school districts. Our Canandaigua connect in February saw numerous new students and parents show up!
