Thank you to everyone who joined us for our Care Ministry Volunteer Potluck and for the delicious food you generously brought to share. It was a lively and joyful time together as volunteers visited, laughed, and enjoyed getting to know one another.

Following lunch,  **Nancy B.**  led a short interactive discussion around our theme,  _“Connecting with Others.”_  Around our tables we reflected on questions such as what helps us connect with someone, what can unintentionally send a message of not caring, and how we naturally bring faith into conversation. It was a meaningful reminder that simple connection is at the heart of every care ministry; whether sharing a meal, offering companionship, or giving someone a ride to an appointment.

Thank you to  **Nancy B.**  for leading this time, and to  **Louise V. and Lydia B.**  for helping organize and plan the gathering. And thank you to  **Margie W.**, who I can always count on to capture wonderful photos of the day!
