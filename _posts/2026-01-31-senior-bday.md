## **February 25 - Happy Birthday Bob Ledgerwood!**

Bob is a man who will be 72 years old in February. He has attended the United Church for many years, but is now mostly home bound from MS. He has many Friends from the Church and attends a weekly Bible study. Bob was a Pharmacist at the VA for many years. His Family, including several Grandkids, visit weekly. Bob loves football and music from the 70s. He is proud to talk about the many band concerts he attended and his time living in Los Angeles. Bob is a wonderful Father and Christian, and a blessing to all who know him.
