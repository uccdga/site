_"Sometimes faith is trusting God when we can't see the way or hear His voice. Sometimes God leads in obscurity and asks us to get up and do what we can with what we have. Sometimes the greatest step of faith is into the unknown, knowing only that Yahweh's hesed (loyal love) will never cease to be present for his people.”_

Try this breath prayer to help center your heart, mind and faith on God, as you look to Him for guidance:  
**_Breath Prayer:_**  (inhale)  _Jesus, my Redeemer_, (exhale)  _guide my steps._
