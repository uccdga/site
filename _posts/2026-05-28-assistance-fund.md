Loose change and other designated gifts will be collected on the **first Sunday in June** to support the **Stewards Assistance Fund**, helping provide compassionate assistance to individuals and families experiencing unexpected needs. In recent months, this fund has enabled us to come alongside several people during difficult seasons, offering practical support at just the right time. Those receiving assistance have expressed deep gratitude for the kindness of their church family. Thank you for helping us continue this caring ministry.
